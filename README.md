## 🧾 Project Summary

* **Industry:** Manufacturing / Food & Beverage
* **Project Type:** Digital Transformation
* **Role:** Business Analyst
* **Delivery Context:** Agile-informed (iterative delivery with stakeholder feedback loops)

---

## 🚨 Business Problem

Rapid business growth exposed critical operational gaps:

* Inconsistent production output
* Poor demand forecasting
* Manual and error-prone processes
* Lack of real-time data visibility
* Misalignment between Sales and Operations

This resulted in missed targets, customer dissatisfaction, and increased operational costs.

---

## 🧠 Approach & Methodology

The engagement followed structured BA practices aligned with **BABOK and Agile principles**:

* Conducted **stakeholder interviews and workshops**
* Performed **process mapping (As-Is)** to identify inefficiencies
* Carried out **gap analysis and root cause analysis**
* Defined **To-Be processes** aligned to business objectives
* Elicited and documented **functional and non-functional requirements**
* Applied **MoSCoW prioritisation** for delivery planning

---

## 👥 Stakeholder Management

Worked closely with cross-functional stakeholders:

* Business Owner (Sponsor)
* Operations Manager (Process Owner)
* Sales Manager (Key User)
* Head Brewer (SME)
* IT Lead (Technical Owner)

### Key Activities

* Facilitated workshops to align on problem definition
* Managed conflicting priorities between Sales and Operations
* Ensured continuous stakeholder engagement throughout

## 🔍 Current State Analysis (As-Is)

Key findings from process analysis:

* Demand planning based on intuition rather than data
* Inventory tracked via spreadsheets with no real-time updates
* Manual fermentation and quality tracking
* No formal reporting or performance metrics
* Reactive equipment maintenance

## 🧩 Requirements Engineering

### Functional Requirements

* Demand forecasting using historical sales data
* Production scheduling aligned to capacity and inventory
* Real-time inventory management and alerts
* Order validation against live stock levels
* Recipe version control and batch traceability
* Equipment maintenance scheduling and fault logging
* Operational dashboards and reporting

### Non-Functional Requirements

* **Performance:** <3 second response time
* **Availability:** 99.5% uptime
* **Security:** Role-based access control
* **Compliance:** UK GDPR
* **Scalability:** Up to 200 concurrent users
* **Usability:** Minimal training required

---

## 💡 Solution Overview

Proposed implementation of an **Integrated Brewery Management Platform** to:

* Provide a **single source of truth**
* Enable **real-time operational visibility**
* Automate key workflows across production and sales
* Improve collaboration between departments
* Support future system integrations (API-enabled architecture)
